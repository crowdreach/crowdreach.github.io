About

A look at our journey so far.

  • MARCH 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our talents merged when we met on the Team entrepreneurship course at UWE, Bristol. With a drive and desire to create our own business we made it our mission to take on all the opportunities that came our way. A chance meeting in the UNI's cafeteria landed us the opportunity to help a robotics company crowdfund their new idea.

  • June 2014

    CrowdReach Was Born

    After two months of preparation, our first campaign launched with amazing success. The strategy worked and the target was reached in under 11 hours and by the end of the campaign it had more than tripled. It was incredible to see backers from all over the world engage and advocated the campaign. Seeing the opportunity to help other businesses and entrepreneurs in the same position, CrowdReach was born.

  • Sept 2014 - June 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    For the next 9 months our minds dived into the depths of crowdfunding. We interviewed keys players in the industry and researched the most successful strategies to break down exactly what makes a campaign successful. With this information we began shaping our own strategy that would be integrated into the full service for our future clients.

  • July 2015

    We're Ready

    Entering our final year, we have the team, time and resources ready to tackle the crowdfunding scene.
